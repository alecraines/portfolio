Research Proposal A Proposal to Research Food Insecurity in Seattle

Project Description

To prepare us for future research, I was tasked with preparing a sample research proposal about a topic of our choosing. My proposal dealt with where and why food deserts are located and why.

Project Details

  • Formatted and made in the style of Research Proposals.
  • What is the relationship between a location's average income on the amount of grocery stores ore fast food restaurants in an area?
  • Pulled resources from research from similar topics.
  • Can lead to overall health improvements of the city.
