Fast Food and Grocery Atlas

Previous Next

Project Description

This was a final research project for my classes. My topic was to find where and why fast food and grocery stores locate where they do. There are some very clear trends.

Project Details

  • Maps created in ArcMap
  • All data was self-gathered off of fast food websites then geocoded in ArcMap
  • Used buffers to illustrate areas that are not in a food desert
  • Theissen polygons are used to show the amount of area that each grocery store serves
