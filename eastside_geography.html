Geography of the Seattle Eastside Through Multiple Perspectives

Project Description

The Seattle Eastside is a large, and growing area. This project is a multi-perspective analysis on the Eastside, through the lenses of Political, Economical, Geopolitical geographies, and also looking at the goods, companies and resources available in the Eastside.

Project Details

  • Data gathered from census data, companies, and other sources.
  • Looks at Schools, Jobs, and Political climate of the Eastside.
  • Discusses the impact of the Cold War in the Eastside.
  • Impact of Boeing, Microsoft on Eastside.
