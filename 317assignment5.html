Markov Transitions and Minorities in King County

Previous Next

Project Description

The Markov Transition lets us illustrate the difference between different levels of an attribute, in this case, percentage of minorities in a census tract. In Map 1, Low is 0-10%, Medium is 11-89%, and High is 90-100%. After the Markov Transition ran, the biggest transition was 164 census tracts went from low to medium. 1127 tracts stayed at medium. In Map 2, Low is 0-25%, Medium is 26-74%, and High is 75-100%, to show the change if we use different categories. From 2000 to 2010, we see a higher transition from low to medium of 271 tracts.

Project Details

  • Maps created in ArcMap
  • Utilized the Markov Transition function in ArcMap
  • Only allowed changes fom High to Medium, Low to Medium, Medium to High, and Medium to Low
