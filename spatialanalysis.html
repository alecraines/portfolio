Spatial Analysis with King County and Post Offices

Previous Next

Project Description

Spatial analysis is important for enhancing maps. In this pair of maps, I use buffers to illustrate possible flood zones in King County and to illustrate the areas that are closest to each post office. Furthermore, in the first map, I see what schools are in the possible flood zones.

Project Details

  • Maps created with ArcMap
  • Used buffers and spatial joins
  • Colored each school by joining the tracts median income to the school
  • Roads are colored with the distance to the nearest post office
