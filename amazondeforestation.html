Deforestation in Brazil

Project Description

This map looks at the possible effects of a roadway being put in through the middle of a stretch of forest. Analysis of past roads show 46% of 5.5km around every road gets deforested. If the proposed road does get built, there is a possible 612 square miles of new deforestation.

Project Details

  • Map made in ArcMap
  • Analyzed the deforested areas around roads to determine how much of the forest gets removed
  • Used buffers to show the areas around the proposed roads getting deforested
  • Traced the road off of a proposal into ArcMap
