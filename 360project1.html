Creating a Story with Data The Economic Climate around UW

Project Description

Using three maps, I created a story about the economic climate around the University of Washington. I did this by looking at median household income, average rent, percentage of houses rented out, and the number of unemployed people, all by census tract. We see that around the UW, there is high rent but low income. Most homes are rented out and there is a large amount of unemployed people in these areas. All of this leads to a bad economic climate for students to live in.

Project Details

  • Maps and Story create in ArcGIS Online
  • Utilized public datasets
  • Seperated by census tracts
  • Faded out neighborhoods over one neighborhood away from the University to keep focus around the University.
